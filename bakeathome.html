VANILLA MUFFINS

Deliciously baked home made muffins!

** Bonus ** Authentic Recipe for you to try baking at home :)

INGREDIENTS

  1. 4 cups/480 grams all-purpose flour
  2. 2 cups granulated sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon plus 2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. 3/4 teaspoon salt
  5. 2 cups milk
  6. 1/2 cup butter, melted
  7. 2 large eggs
  8. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract or 1/4 teaspoon vanilla bean paste

STEPS TO MAKE IT

01 Gather the ingredients.

02 Preheat the oven to 400 F. Grease 24 muffin cups or line them with paper cupcake liners.

03 In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Stir to mix thoroughly.

04 In a medium bowl, whisk together the milk, butter, eggs, and vanilla extract or paste until combined.

05 Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Use a spoon to mix thoroughly.

06 Spoon into greased or paper-lined muffin tins.

07 Bake in the preheated oven for about 15 to 20 minutes. A dark pan will take less time than a light colored pan. The muffins are done when a toothpick or cake tester comes out clean when inserted in the middle of a muffin. Remove the muffins from the tin and let cool on a cooling rack. Serve and enjoy!
